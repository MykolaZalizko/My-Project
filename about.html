About Me

My Homeland

I was born in Kyiv, Ukraine. I grew up here, graduated from school here and became a person also here. This city is a capital of Ukraine. A lot of majestic architecture, colourful parks, historical museums and places, all of these is about Kyiv. Unfortunately, after the beginning of the war, this place became more dark and grey, but I believe that after the end of it, Kyiv will shine like never before.
